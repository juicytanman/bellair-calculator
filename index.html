How to read this

Cash jobs keep 100% of the money after materials — no tax, no GST. To match that take-home on a proper invoiced job, you need to gross up the profit for income tax, then add GST on top. Materials pass through at cost (just GST added), so the more materials in a job, the closer the multiplier gets to 1.1×. Pure labour jobs sit closer to 1.5×.
